# Roadmap

## 🚀 Advanced RPG Phase (Post-MVP)

Moving forward, the architecture will be heavily focused on single-player scalability, emphasizing modular skill progression, interactive resource hubs, and deep crafting loops inspired by RuneScape and Stardew Valley.

---

### v1.3 — 🏦 Centralized Bank System (Current)
Focus on creating a central hub for item storage, removing the need for scattered chests.
- Centralized Bank system replacing chests.
- Global persistent storage accessible at specific locations.

### v1.2 — 🖱️ Point-and-Click & Interactions (Done)
Focus on modernizing the control scheme to feel more like an authentic RPG.
- Grid-based pathfinding for point-and-click movement.
- Context-sensitive mouse interactions (left click to interact/move).
- Dropping items from inventory to the ground.

### v1.1 — 🏡 Agriculture & Storage (Done)
Focus on expanding the player's interactions with the environment and improving item management.
- Grid-based farming mechanics.
- Placeable storage chests.

### Future Milestones (To Be Defined)
- Tick-based combat and action queuing.
- Skill Requirements and Tool Tiering.
- Mini-map for navigation.
- Further skill expansions (e.g. Fishing, Cooking).
- Quests and NPC interactions.
- Advanced enemy behaviors and boss encounters.
- World expansion (new biomes, dungeons).
